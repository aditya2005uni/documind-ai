    Under the Hood

    How DocuMind AI works

    A simple, transparent pipeline from raw PDF to trustworthy answer.

    1

    Upload

    Your PDF is parsed and split into meaningful chunks of text.

    2

    Embed & Store

    Each chunk is converted into vector embeddings and stored for fast semantic search.

    3

    Retrieve

    When you ask a question, the most relevant chunks are retrieved from your document.

    4

    Answer

    The LLM answers using only the retrieved context — or tells you it's not in the document.
